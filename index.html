Services

How I help

Six practical pillars that stabilize reporting, organize data, and make AI adoption realistic and repeatable.

Fractional Data & Analytics Leadership

I act as part-time senior leadership: set direction, prioritize initiatives, and coach teams. Executives get experienced oversight without a full-time hire. That leadership keeps delivery on track and transfers capability to your organization.

Data & Analytics Strategy

I translate business priorities into a pragmatic analytics and AI roadmap with clear milestones. This ensures investments produce measurable outcomes instead of scattered projects. The roadmap ties short-term wins to long-term value.

Modern Reporting & BI

I consolidate reports, standardize dashboards, and implement simple governance so teams use one version of the truth. Executives get reliable KPIs; teams spend less time firefighting. That stability increases confident, timely decision-making.

Data Quality & Governance

I define ownership, KPI definitions, and lightweight controls that restore trust in your numbers. Practical governance reduces rework and political disputes over data. It protects analytics investments and supports scaling AI safely.

AI Readiness & Enablement

I assess readiness, prioritize safe pilot use cases, and prepare the data, controls, and evaluation processes models require. Executives get a clear path to test and scale AI while managing risk. This turns AI from experiment to repeatable business value.

Data Estate Inventory & Curation

I catalog databases, ETL pipelines, reports, and dataset owners; rationalize duplicates and curate trusted sources. Leaders gain visibility into what data exists and where quality issues live. Curated datasets accelerate analytics and reduce manual Excel work.

Approach

Three-step method

A simple, business-focused approach that balances speed, risk, and sustainable value.

Step 1
Discovery & Assessment
Stakeholder interviews, KPI and process review, a focused data estate inventory (sources, pipelines, reports, owners), and a data quality diagnostic to surface high-risk gaps.
Step 2
Strategy & Roadmap
Define target state for analytics and AI, prioritize use cases tied to measurable outcomes, and produce a pragmatic 90-day and 12-month roadmap across people, process, and technology.
Step 3
Execution & Ongoing Leadership
Execute prioritized work, provide fractional or project leadership, and coach your team to embed sustainable practices so value endures after the engagement.
