What Cleveland Strategic Can Help You Do

Services

Fractional leadership and practical consulting for organizations that need a Head of Data & Analytics to stabilize reporting, organize the data estate, and get ready for AI.

Fractional Data & Analytics Leadership
Act as your part-time Head of Data & Analytics, partnering with finance, operations, and IT to set priorities, unblock teams, and keep work aligned with the strategy. Gives you executive-level oversight without the cost and risk of a full-time hire.
Data & Analytics Strategy
Clarify where data, analytics, and AI should support the business, define the most important use cases, and translate them into a roadmap your teams can actually execute. Connects investments in data to measurable business outcomes.
Modern Reporting & BI
Reduce reliance on fragile spreadsheets and one-off slide decks by moving to governed dashboards and a smaller set of trusted reports. Frees up your team from manual reporting so they can focus on higher-value analysis and AI use cases.
Data Quality & Governance
Establish ownership, definitions, and simple controls for your most important data and KPIs. Shifts leadership conversations away from "which number is right?" toward "what decision do we make?" and lays the groundwork for safe, reliable AI.
AI Readiness & Enablement
Assess where AI can realistically add value today, what data it depends on, and what guardrails you need. Aligns AI experiments with business priorities and ensures your data, governance, and risk posture can support AI instead of fighting it.
Data Estate Inventory & Curation
Catalog and rationalize databases, pipelines, and reports so you know what you have, what is trusted, and what should be retired. Reduces duplication and clutter while creating curated datasets that analytics and AI teams can safely build on.

Results You Can Expect

Outcomes

Every organization starts from a different place, but engagements typically drive outcomes like:

  • A clear analytics and AI roadmap tied directly to revenue, cost, and risk goals.
  • A stable, trusted reporting environment where executives see the same numbers everywhere.
  • Consistent KPI definitions with clear owners, documentation, and business context.
  • A well-documented data estate, with clarity on which sources, pipelines, and reports actually matter.
  • Decommissioned or consolidated legacy reports and dashboards that were creating noise and duplicate work.
  • Curated datasets designed for analytics and AI use cases, instead of ad-hoc extracts and one-off tables.
  • Reduced dependence on a few “Excel heroes” and fewer fire drills to explain conflicting metrics.
  • A realistic, risk-aware path to adopting AI that matches the maturity of your data foundations.

How We Work Together

Approach

Simple structure, clear expectations, and plain language. No buzzword-heavy decks or vague “transformations.”

Step 1
Discovery & Assessment

Stakeholder interviews, review of your current reporting, and a quick diagnostic of data quality and governance. Includes a focused data estate inventory to understand key sources, pipelines, and reports and where trust is breaking down.

Step 2
Strategy & Roadmap

Define the target state for reporting, analytics, and AI, then prioritize a practical 90-day and 12-month roadmap. Links specific analytics and AI use cases to the data, governance, and technical work required so everyone understands the trade-offs.

Step 3
Execution & Ongoing Leadership

Provide fractional leadership, embedded with your teams as needed, to drive delivery, coach staff, and keep decisions anchored in business outcomes. Ensures that reporting, data estate cleanup, and AI pilots move forward together instead of in silos.
